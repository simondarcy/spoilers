After months of speculation Rob Donovan was revealed as Tina McIntyre's killer in last night's (May 27) dramatic episode of Coronation Street.

The Corrie charmer, played by Marc Baylis, was responsible for Tina's fall from the balcony above Owen's builders' yard, as a huge row between them accidentally ended in tragedy.

Following months of speculation over the identity of Tina's murder, the episode saw Rob follow Tina to the top of the balcony where a violent tussle between the pair ended in her falling to the cobbles below.

Although Rob didn't intend to hurt Tina, the accident will lead to her passing away in hospital next week.

A huge row broke out between the pair as Tina threatened to reveal the truth about her affair with Rob's sister's husband, Peter Barlow.

Rob wanted to spare Carla the heartbreak of finding out the truth about her cheating husband, but his intervention was in vain as Peter came clean to her about the affair before anyone else could.

Coronation Street bosses managed to prevent any leaks about the killer's identity until transmission last night, despite media attention around the storyline.

On tonight's episode which airs at 9pm on UTV and TV3, viewers will see Tina rushed to hospital after the residents find her motionless body.
